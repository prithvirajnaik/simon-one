Press a key to Start

The goal is to correctly repeat an increasingly long sequence of lights and sounds.
Simon will play a sequence starting with one tone and light.
You must press the buttons in the same order as Simon's sequence.
After each correct sequence, Simon will add one more tone and light to the sequence.
The sequence continues to grow as long as you keep repeating it correctly.
Wait for Simon to finish the sequence before pressing any buttons.
Repeat the sequence exactly as it was shown.
If you press the wrong button or the wrong order, Simon will emit a "fail" sound and the game will end.
